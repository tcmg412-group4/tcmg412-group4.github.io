Hello, welcome to my website!

The purpose of this website is to show you about who I am, what I do, and what I want my future to be. In this website you will find information about me, about what has been my work life and interesting facts about my life. In the main page you will find information about me, including my background and what I am doing right now. In the Work History section, you will find information about what my work life has been so far. Information to get in contact with me and a copy of my resume will also be included in the section. In the My life section you will find more information and facts about my life. I talk about my current and future goals, my college career, and interesting past and present facts about myself. In the website I included images that represent some of the most important times in my life.

My name is Giovanni Hernandez Cuevas. I was born on July 10th, 1997 in San Luis Potosi, Mexico. I lived my whole life in Mexico until I started my college education in 2018. I applied for an International Student Visa to study Computer Science at Lone Star College North Harris, which is located in Houston, Texas. I graduated from Lone Star College in Fall 2019, I obtained a Associate of Science Degree in Computer Science. After I graduated from Lone Star College I transferred to Texas A&M University in College Station, Texas. I transferred to start the Spring 2020 semester to major in Technology Management; it has been one of the best experiences I have had so far in my life.
