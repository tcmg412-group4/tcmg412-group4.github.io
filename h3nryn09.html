Shark Facts

Some sharks need to constantly move to stay alive

Sharks do not have bones, but can still fossilize

Sharks have lived for a long time, dating back to dinosaurs

The Whale Shark is the largest living fish on the planet

Sharks can have up to 300 or more teeth

You are more likely to be killed by fireworks than a shark

There are over 500 species of sharks

If a shark is flipped upside down, they go into a trance state
